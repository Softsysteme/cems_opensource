Welcome on the Demo Package login page
The salesforce login widget is already configured to be connected on a sample username and password.
You can see them by clicking the     icon on the top of the login widget (not available with "Guest" authentication). Do not modify these setting as the demo will not work any more.

When SalesForce login will be completed in the left datawidget, you will be proposed to initialize this account for the demo. DO NOT click the Initialize button displayed in the right datawidget as this will cause the demo data to be reset.
